The von Neumann Model

提出程式和數據在邏輯上相同，應存儲在計算機的記憶體中

基於馮·諾依曼模型構建的計算機將計算機硬體劃分為五個子系統：
輸入單元 （IU）
記憶體單元 （MU）
算術邏輯單元 （ALU）
控制單元 （CU）
輸出單元 （OU）

※ Central Processing Unit (CPU): MU + ALU + CU ※ Processing Unit (PU) : ALU + CU

Computer Systems

Analog vs. Digital systems

Analog Systems

to work on numerical data represented by some physical quantity (e.g. rotation or displacement) or electrical quantity (e.g. voltage, current, resistance, inductance, capacitance, or charge) which varies continuously, in contrast to digital signals which are either 0 or 1.



Digital Systems

represents numbers and other data using discrete internal states (e.g., 1/0, Hi/Lo, On/Off, True/False, …), in contrast to the continuously varying quantities used in an analog computer.
